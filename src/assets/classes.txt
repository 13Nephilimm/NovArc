1. Berserker
Benefits:
- Extremely high physical damage
- Rage mechanic increases damage as health decreases
- Can break enemy defenses

Weaknesses:
- Low defense and survivability
- Limited ranged abilities
- Hard to control in prolonged fights

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. Sentinel
Benefits:
- High health and defense
- Can taunt enemies to protect allies
- Strong against physical attacks

Weaknesses:
- Low energy and damage output
- Weak against magic attacks
- Slow and less mobile

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. Speedster
Benefits:
- Extremely fast, can attack multiple times per turn
- High dodge and hit chance
- Great for critical hits and finishing blows

Weaknesses:
- Low health and defense
- Cannot sustain heavy attacks
- Energy drains fast with multiple abilities

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Mage
Benefits:
- High energy for spells
- Can hit multiple enemies
- Strong magical damage

Weaknesses:
- Low health
- Vulnerable in close combat
- Requires careful energy management

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Elemental
Benefits:
- Can control fire, ice, or lightning for powerful effects
- High versatility against different enemy types
- Can inflict status effects (burn, freeze, stun)

Weaknesses:
- Medium health and defense
- Damage may be weaker if enemy resists element
- High energy cost for big elemental attacks

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. Ranger
Benefits:
- Excellent long-range damage
- High critical chance
- Can set traps or use ranged tactics

Weaknesses:
- Medium health, weak in close combat
- Moderate energy
- Limited melee abilities

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. Summoner
Benefits:
- Can summon creatures/minions to fight
- Flexible: can focus on offense or defense via summons
- Can control battlefield indirectly

Weaknesses:
- Low personal damage
- Summons require energy to maintain
- Vulnerable when summons are gone

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. Engineer
Benefits:
- Can deploy gadgets, turrets, or traps
- Moderate damage and support abilities
- Good utility for team buffs or crowd control

Weaknesses:
- Medium health and energy
- Limited direct attack
- Needs setup time for maximum efficiency
